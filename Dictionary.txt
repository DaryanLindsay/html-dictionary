the html dictionary

h2-6: makes text big but smaller than header. atttribute: none. example: <h2>3 things I like</h2>

img: Makes an image appear on page. attribute: src. example: <img src="beagle.jpg" height="281" width="312">
li: makes a list. attribute: none. example: <li>tavelling</li>

</ol>
